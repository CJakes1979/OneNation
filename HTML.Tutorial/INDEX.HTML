About

Frontend Simplified is a community that focuses on teaching people how to code and design beautiful websites. We have a variety of resources available, including tutorials, articles, and a Discord server where you can ask questions and get help from other members of the community.

Whether you're a complete beginner or an experienced developer looking to improve your skills, Frontend Simplified has something for you. Our tutorials cover a wide range of topics, from HTML and CSS to JavaScript and React, so you can learn at your own pace and focus on the areas that interest you most.

Join our community today and start learning how to create stunning websites that will impress your friends, family, and potential employers!
